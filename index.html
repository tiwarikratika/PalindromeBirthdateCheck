Check out if your Birthdate is Palindrome

Wondering what a palindrome is?
A palindrome is a word/number which reads the same forward and backward. For example: 112211.

Let's find out

Enter your birthdate and we
will tell you if your birthdate
is a palindrome

This app checks your birthdate in 4 formats yyyy-mm-dd,
dd-mm-yyyy,mm-dd-yyyy, m-dd-yyyyy

e.g. if your birthdate is 01 Aug 1995, then app will check for 19950801,01081995,080195, 108195.
